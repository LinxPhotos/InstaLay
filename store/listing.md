## Full description

Insta Lay (also searched as instalay, instacrop, instasize, instafit, instazoom) is the batch photo frame and Instagram layout tool for people who are tired of forced crops.

Prepare photos for Instagram feed, Stories, and Reels with the aspect ratios that actually matter — especially Instagram’s 4:5 portrait limit — without chopping faces, landscapes, or product shots.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Batch edit: import many photos, apply one canvas, preview every thumbnail with the matte applied
• Choose aspect ratio: 4:5, 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 3:2, 2:3, 21:9
• Fine-tune border thickness in pixels — photo frame / insta frame / letterbox / pillarbox
• Fit, cover, or fill — nocrop / no-crop / no crop friendly contain mode by default
• Background swatches: sheer whites, light greys, taupes, and cinematic darks grouped by photographic zones (plus all white and all black). Default is a very sheer light grey
• Photographic paper textures: fine grain, matte fiber, cold press, baryta
• High-quality downsampling: Lanczos-3 or Lanczos-2 for thumbnails and exports (also cubic, linear, nearest)
• 1:1 preview sidebar so you can inspect the real export pixels
• Tapestry / panorama / scroll mode inspired by SCRL — stitch photos into a carousel at any supported ratio
• Save canvas configurations as templates for consistent results across photo sets
• Projects saved on device with version history — posting to Instagram freezes that version; clone to keep editing or repost older exports
• Share / post toward Instagram from your layout list
